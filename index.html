About Naksis AI Video Bot

Naksis AI Video Bot is an AI-powered platform that allows users to create short videos from text prompts and publish approved content directly to their own social media accounts.

The application integrates with TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API to enable secure authentication and user-authorized content publishing.
